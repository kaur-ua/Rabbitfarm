About RabbitFarm

RabbitFarm is a rabbit farm management system created by a rabbit breeder for rabbit breeders.

Our Story

RabbitFarm started from a real rabbit farm and a simple problem: keeping track of breeding, kindling, vaccinations and important dates using notebooks and paper records.

As the number of rabbits increased, keeping records became more difficult and mistakes became easier to make. RabbitFarm was created to provide a simple and practical way to organize farm information and keep important events under control.

Current Features

  • Rabbit records
  • Group management
  • Event tracking
  • Breeding management
  • Kindling planning
  • Vaccination scheduling
  • Farm dashboard

Development

RabbitFarm is actively developing. New features and improvements are added based on practical farm experience and breeder feedback.

The goal of RabbitFarm is to help rabbit breeders spend less time on paperwork and more time managing their farms.
